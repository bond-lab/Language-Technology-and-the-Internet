These are selected papers for Assignment One (analyze a medium of communication) of HG2052: Language, Technology and the Internet. They are roughly grouped by medium, in no particular order. Particularly good papers are marked with ⍟. We hope that they will serve as an inspiration to others. Note that the date is the date of the assignment, not the medium.
